Interesting facts about Camel

  1. Camel's ears are furry.
  2. Camels have three sets of eyelids.
  3. They can reach upto 7 feets in height.
  4. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny planys other animals can't eat.

Interesting facts about Chameleon

  • Chameleons are believed to have existed 55 to 66 million years ago.
  • Skin crystals enable them to change colour at will.
  • Chameleon's feet work like salad tongs.
  • They are not able to swim.

Interesting facts about Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are highly sociable birds.
  2. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent birds.
  3. Pigeons can move their wings ten times per second.
  4. They can fly upto 6000 feet.

Interesting facts about Teddy Bear

  • Teddy Bears are named after Theodore Roosevelt, 26th president of US.
  • First teddy bear went on sale on February of 1903.
  • Animation movie giant Walt Disney produced the colour cartoon film featuring teddy bears.
  • The first British Teddy Bear Festival was held in 1989 in London.

Interesting facts about Panda

  1. Giant Pandas are good at climbing and swimming.
  2. Pandas are lazy animals.
  3. An adult panda can eat 12 to 38 kilos of bamboo every day.
  4. Although pandas are clumsy but they are very intelligent.

Interesting facts about Cicada

  • Cicadas live on all continents except Antartica.
  • Female cicadas may be attracted to the sound of motors.
  • Most of cicadas have orange and red eyes.
  • Their circadian rhytms are still mysterious.
